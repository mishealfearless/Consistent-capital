2.0 Definitions and Interpretation

In these Terms:

  • Borrower refers to any individual or entity applying for or receiving a loan from CONSISTENT CAPITALS LIMITED.
  • Investor refers to any individual or entity providing capital to CONSISTENT CAPITALS LIMITED for investment purposes.
  • Collateral refers to any tangible or intangible asset provided by the Borrower as security for a loan.
  • Salary Earner refers to a Borrower employed in a capacity where regular salary income is earned.
  • Entrepreneur refers to a Borrower engaged in self-employment or business activities, not receiving a regular salary from a third-party employer.
The headings in this document are for reference purposes only and shall not affect the interpretation of the clauses herein.

3.0 Eligibility to Access Services

To be eligible for the Company’s services, including loans or investment opportunities, the Client must:

  • Be at least 18 years of age and possess full legal capacity to enter into a binding agreement.
  • Provide accurate, up-to-date identification and any additional documentation as may be requested by the Company.
  • Not be an undischarged bankrupt or subject to insolvency proceedings.

4.0 Loan Application and Approval Process

4.1 Written Agreement Requirement

Prior to the disbursement of any loan, the Borrower shall execute a formal Loan Agreement, which shall clearly outline the applicable interest rate, loan tenure, repayment schedule, and any additional terms as may be required. No loan shall be disbursed without a duly executed agreement.

4.2 Collateral Requirement for Entrepreneurs

Entrepreneurs, or any Borrower who is not classified as a Salary Earner, shall be required to provide adequate Collateral as security for any loan. Acceptable forms of Collateral may include, but are not limited to:

  • Real property (e.g., land, buildings, houses)
  • Motor vehicles
  • Precious metals, such as gold bars
  • Jewelry
  • Electronics (e.g., laptops, mobile phones)

The Borrower must provide verifiable proof of ownership of the Collateral, and the Company reserves the right to conduct any necessary due diligence, including valuation, to ascertain the adequacy and legality of the Collateral. Collateral that is encumbered by existing debts or other legal restrictions will not be accepted.

5.0 Post-Dated Cheques for Salary Earners

Borrowers who are Salary Earners may submit post-dated cheques from their salary account to serve as a guarantee for loan repayment. It is expressly provided that:

  • Such cheques must be drawn from a current account only. The Company shall not accept post-dated cheques from savings accounts as they are not valid for clearing under current banking regulations in Nigeria.
  • he cheque must be from a financial institution where the Company also holds an account or otherwise has a clearing agreement.

6.0 Default and Enforcement of Collateral

6.1 Default

A Borrower shall be considered in default if they fail to comply with any of the repayment terms outlined in the Loan Agreement. In the event of a default, CONSISTENT CAPITALS LIMITED reserves the right to:

  • Take legal action to recover the outstanding loan amount.
  • Enforce the sale of any Collateral provided in accordance with Nigerian law to recover the loan.

6.2 Enforcement of Collateral

Upon the occurrence of a default, CONSISTENT CAPITALS LIMITED shall be entitled to take possession of the Collateral and liquidate the asset(s) in compliance with all relevant legal procedures. The net proceeds from such sale shall be applied towards settling the outstanding loan, including any accrued interest, penalties, and costs associated with the enforcement.
The Borrower acknowledges and agrees that the Company shall not be liable for any delay in enforcing the Collateral due to procedural requirements under Nigerian law.

6.3 Dishonored Cheques

In the event that any post-dated cheque submitted by a Salary Earner is dishonored, the Company reserves the right to initiate both civil and criminal proceedings, as provided under Nigerian law, to recover the debt and any associated penalties.

7.0 Investor Capital Protection

7.1 Investor Capital

Investors’ capital is secured by the Collateral provided by Borrowers, as outlined in Clause 3.2. In the event of a default by a Borrower, CONSISTENT CAPITALS LIMITED will enforce its rights to the Collateral in order to recover the Investor's capital.

7.2 Recovery and Timeframes

Investors are informed that the return of their capital in the event of Borrower default is contingent upon the successful enforcement and sale of the Collateral. The Company shall use its best efforts to complete this process promptly, but makes no guarantees as to the timeframe required for such recovery, as this may be subject to external factors such as court processes.

7.3 Capital Only Guarantee

While the Company will take all reasonable steps to recover the Investor’s capital, it is expressly provided that CONSISTENT CAPITALS LIMITED only guarantees the return of the principal investment amount. Any interest or anticipated returns are dependent on the repayment by Borrowers and successful Collateral recovery.

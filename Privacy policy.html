Privacy Policy | Consistent Capitals

1.0 Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes our policies and procedures on the collection, use and disclosure of your information when you use the services and tells you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, you may choose to not use our Service. This policy may change from time to time (Refer to section 12 on ‘Changes to this Privacy Policy’) We use your Personal data to provide and improve the Service. By accessing or using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information under this Privacy Policy.

2.0 Applicability

This Privacy Policy applies to:

  • Any individual ("Client") who uses our services, including loans, investments, or any interaction with us via our platforms (website, social media, etc.).
  • Visitors to our website or any of our digital platforms.
  • Any person who contacts us for inquiries, complaints, or contractual purposes

3.0 Consent

By accessing our website, utilizing our services, or engaging with us, you consent to the processing of your data under this Privacy Policy. You have the right to withdraw your consent at any time by contacting us. However, withdrawal of consent may affect the ability to provide you with certain services.

Collecting and Using Your Personal Data.

4.0 Types of Data Collected

We collect and process the following categories of personal data:

  • Identity Data: Name, gender, date of birth, BVN (Bank Verification Number), and employment details
  • Contact Data: Address, email address, and phone number.
  • Financial Data: Bank account details, loan, and investment information.
  • Transaction Data: Records of your payments and other financial transactions with us.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and information about how you interact with our website.
  • Usage Data: Information on your use of our digital platforms, including website interactions and transaction history.
  • Marketing and Communications Data: Preferences for receiving marketing materials and feedback on our services.
We may also collect additional data from third-party service providers, such as credit reference agencies, to verify your identity, assess your creditworthiness, and perform due diligence.

5.0 Use of Your Personal Data

The Company may use Personal Data for the following purposes:

  • To process and manage your loan or investment applications.
  • To execute and manage contracts, including Loan Agreements and Investment Contracts.
  • To verify your identity and perform creditworthiness assessments.
  • To communicate with you regarding your transactions, inquiries, and account updates.
  • To comply with legal obligations, including KYC (Know Your Customer) and AML (Anti-Money Laundering) requirements.
  • To ensure the security and functionality of our platforms.
  • For marketing purposes, where you have opted in to receive such communications.
  • To perform data analytics to improve our products and services.

6.0 Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data under the following legal grounds:

  • Consent:When you voluntarily provide data for specific purposes, such as marketing.
  • Contractual necessity:To fulfil our contractual obligations to you, including disbursing loans and managing investments.
  • Legal obligations: to comply with applicable Nigerian laws and regulations, including those related to money lending and financial services.
  • Legitimate interests:For internal business purposes such as fraud prevention, risk management, and improving our services.

7.0 Data Sharing

We may share your personal data with the following categories of third parties:

  • Service Providers: IT service providers, financial advisors, and other partners who help us deliver our services
  • Regulatory Authorities: As required to comply with laws or regulatory requirements.
  • Credit Agencies: To assess creditworthiness or for risk assessment purposes.
  • Legal Representatives In the case of legal claims or litigation related to your account or loan.
We ensure that any third parties with whom we share your data uphold strict data protection standards and process your data only by our instructions.

8.0 Data Retention

We will retain your data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected. Typically, we will retain customer-related information for six (6) years after the closure of your account or the completion of any contractual obligations, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

9.0 Security Measures

We have implemented robust technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. These measures include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data.
  • Access control protocols.
  • Regular audits and security assessments.
  • Staff training on data protection and privacy regulations.
However, please be aware that no data transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

10.0 Your Rights

As a data subject, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • AccessYou may request access to your data held by us.
  • CorrectionYou may request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • DeletionYou may request the deletion of your data, subject to legal or contractual obligations.
  • RestrictionYou may request the restriction of processing under certain circumstances.
  • Data PortabilityYou may request to receive a copy of your data in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Withdrawal of Consent:You may withdraw consent to data processing at any time, provided that the processing was based on your consent.
To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at; dataprotection@consistentcapitals.com.

11.0 Marketing Communications

You can opt out of receiving marketing communications from us by following the instructions in each marketing email or by contacting us directly. Please note that opting out of marketing communications will not affect the delivery of important transactional or service-related communications.

12.0 Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and, where appropriate, we will notify you through other communication channels. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy reflects the most recent changes

13.0 Contact Information and Complaints

If you have any questions, concerns, or complaints regarding how we handle your data,
you may contact us at dataprotection@consistentcapitals.com
